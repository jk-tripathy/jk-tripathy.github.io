Research
Pattern Prediction using LSTM and ReLU Ensemble
A work mainly intiaed to find the patterns occuring in the daily activities of humans. The work uses LSTM which has demostrated having significant skill in being able to remember context and history in NLP. This model performed extermely well and gave an accuracy of 80%.

This work got accepted in the ICICC-2020 conference and was further published in SSRN
Finetuning for Cyberbullying
To offer a stand against cyberbullying, this work uses more recent advancements in the field of NLP like the ALBERT model for the use of finetuning classifcations of hate speech found online. Fine tuned on of the largest dataset containing hate speec, the ALBERT based fine tuning apprach gave an F1 score of 95%.

This work has been accepted in Springer Multimedia Systems
Review on NLP Techniques
This review paper discusses the different approaches that are used for the sequence to sequence mapping in Natural Language Processing. From the inception of the field to more recent advances such as large pretrained models.

Work done for Undergradute Research Experience course and while working for the Artificial Intelligence Research Lab, VIT-AP.
